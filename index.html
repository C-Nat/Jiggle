Why jelly, not biscuits?

Made for dry mouth, soreness & taste changes

Chemo can make everything taste wrong. Mouths feel dry, sore, and every bite of a biscuit feels like work. Jiggle is built as a small kindness for those days when you still need protein, but chewing is the last thing you want to do.

Soft, slippery texture
Jelly drink slides instead of scraping. Less friction on sore mouths and throats.
Flavours that cut through chemo taste
Bright lemon salt and gentle coconut are chosen to be refreshing, not overpowering, to work around distorted taste without feeling artificial.
Small, meaningful serving
100 g is enough to matter nutritionally, but small enough to finish even on low-appetite days, whether in hospital or at home.

Jiggle is a concept product designed to explore a kinder, high-protein snack for athletes, everyday lives and people receiving chemotherapy. Not medical advice: final formulations should always be checked with healthcare teams and dietitians for safety and suitability.
